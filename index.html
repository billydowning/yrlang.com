Why YrLang?

It's likely that you will one day find yourself in a foreign country without proper knowledge of the local language (if you haven't already). Where do you begin your journey and how can you be certain you will end up where you would like to be?
‍

Flashback to 2018 - to our adventure as US expats in Berlin. We had learned some Deutsch, researched the city, watched EasyGerman, the works. However, nothing quite prepares you for the nuances of everyday life as a non-native speaker - and we figure we are not alone in our challenges, so that's why we created YrLang (think of it as You're Speaking My Language - just a lot easier to say).

Our Blog, The Brücke

We want to share our journey while also helping you with yours - that’s why we came up with our blog, The Brücke. It means "bridge" in German and captures the essence of what we see for YrLang.

Its purpose is to help you cross language barriers and establish deeper and more meaningful connections both where you are and where you want to be in the future, all while sharing some of our ‘life’ lessons as former expats in Berlin.
