Why YrLang?

Chances are (if you haven't already) that you will one day find yourself in a foreign country without proper knowledge of the local language. Where do you begin your journey and how can you be certain you will end up where you would like to be at the end of the day? Flashback to 2018, on our adventure as US expats in Berlin -we had learned some Deutsch, researched the city, watched EasyGerman, the works. Nothing quite prepares you for the nuances of everyday life as a non-native - and we figure we are not unique in our challenges. That's why we created YrLang (think of it as You're Speaking My Language -just a lot easier to say).

Our Solution

We're a hub for digital nomads, expats, and just about every kind of traveler. We provide a community space that connects locals with fluent second language skills in multiple foreign cities to our community of adventure seekers/ location-independent/ remote lifers -creating a comfortable space to broaden your travel experiences.

Our Blog, The Brucke

We want to share our journey, and help you along yours -that’s why we came up with our blog, The Brücke. It means bridge in Deutsch and captures the essence of what we see for YrLang -its purpose is to help you cross language barriers and connect more with where you are and where you want to be, while also sharing some of our ‘life’ lessons as former expats in Berlin.
