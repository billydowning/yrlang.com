BECOME A LOCALITE

What is a Localite?

Less like a travel guide/ more like a friend helping you get around (or just get through life) in a new city.

Benefits

  • Earn money while helping others navigate getting around your city
  • Be a global citizen and help share your city’s culture and heritage with others
  • Build your global community one visit at a time without feeling like you need to do the same tour over and over... because every visit (and every traveler) is different

What do Localities do?

Imagine you have a good friend visiting that has never been to your city and doesn’t speak the language -how would you try to make their experience the best it could be? Now imagine your friend decided to move to your city for work, a relationship, or maybe just to hangout for 3-4 months, but they don’t know exactly how and where to go to get settled in.

This is where a Localite can help, they are just a local friend helping someone move through the communication barriers of getting around, getting settled, and everything involved in just living in a new city. Simple things often become difficult when starting up in a new place, like finding a vet for your pets, getting a haircut or a massage, and even setting up new phone service.

We believe in connecting those with fluent or native second language proficiency and a love for their city with travelers worldwide so they can make money while doing what they love, where they love to be.

the Basics:

  • 1

    simply sign-up for a user account with us today

  • 2

    once you receive your User Dashboard, follow the prompts to submit a request (or just click the Become a Localite button)

  • 3

    you will be prompted to complete our online application, review and check-off our Ground Rules doc, upload a short video for every language you would like to verify in (a separate video for each language), and submit.

  • 4

    you will receive a confirmation email with a status update within approx. 1 week of submission (depending on the #s of requests we receive this may be longer). If your video is deemed fluent level or above, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to your Localite dashboard page and you will be prompted to choose a time to schedule a video chat with someone from our Verification Team. Once you book a video chat, you will receive a confirmation email, or an SMS text based on your notification preferences.

  • 2

    you will receive an SMS notification 1-hour prior to your scheduled video chat. The short video conversation will be about 5 minutes and will be with a language professional in the language you are to be verified in. Once completed, you will receive a status update within the same day via your notification preferences. If approved, you will be sent a welcome email and a link to your updated Localite dashboard, along with a link to our contract document to sign-off on.
